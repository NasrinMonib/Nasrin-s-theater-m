About the movie-info

the girl on the train The Girl on the Train is an American psychological thriller film written by Erin Cressida Wilson and directed by Tate Taylor, based on the novel of the same name by British author Paula Hawkins. The film stars Emily Blunt, Rebecca Ferguson, Haley Bennett, Justin Theroux, Luke Evans, and Edgar Ramirez
